The Global Scale for Early Development (GSED)

M.M. Black, K. Bromley, V.A. Cavallera, J. Cuartas, T. Dua, I. Eekhout, G. Fink, M. Gladstone, K. Hepworth, M. Janus, P. Kariger, G. Lancaster, D. McCoy, G. McCray, A. Raikes, M. Rubio-Codina, Stef van Buuren, M. Waldman, S. Walker, A. Weber
May 2019
PDF

Abstract

Countries need good metrics to track their progress towards early childhood development targets. No reliable, freely accessible tools exist for population-level monitoring of children up to age 3. The Global Scale for Early Development, now being field-tested, could offer a global solution.
